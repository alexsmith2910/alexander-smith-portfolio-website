01

Colour

A near-monochrome system. Warm bone paper against soft ink, with a single near-black for immersive dark zones. The only colour lives in the WebGL plasma — a cold violet glow that surfaces during the menu takeover and footer dimming. Never introduce saturated UI colour.

04

Motion

Movement is slow, weighted and continuous. Nothing snaps. Smooth-scroll (Lenis) underpins everything; reveals are lerped to scroll position rather than triggered. Always honour prefers-reduced-motion with instant fallbacks.

05

Voice

Principles
  • Confident, never loud.
  • Short declaratives.
  • Concrete over abstract.
  • The browser is a medium.
  • Atmosphere, not interface.
In practice
Headlines pose a feeling or a question, then land on an italic clause — “…at the edge of the web.” Project meta reads like a file system: numbered, mono, lowercase slugs. Coordinates and build credits sign every footer. Replaceable asset slots are always labelled in brackets.
